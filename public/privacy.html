Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 26, 2025

Welcome to Ukobow. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide our hotel booking and listing services:

  • Personal Information: Name, Email Address, Phone Number.
  • Verification Documents (For Hotel Owners): Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Electricity Bills, Food License (images).
  • Financial Information: Bank Account numbers and IFSC codes for payouts (Hotel Owners only).
  • Location Data: We collect precise or coarse location data to help you find hotels nearby.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process hotel bookings and reservations.
  • To verify the identity of Hotel Partners for safety and legal compliance.
  • To process payments and payouts.
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • To send authentication codes (OTP) via SMS/Email.

3. App Permissions

Our app requires the following permissions to function correctly:

  • Camera & Storage: To allow users and owners to upload profile photos, hotel images, and verification documents.
  • Internet: To connect to our servers and update data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with:

  • Firebase (Google): For secure authentication and phone number verification.
  • MongoDB Atlas: For secure cloud database storage.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by Indian law for compliance and auditing.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures (HTTPS, Secure Database Connections) to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
