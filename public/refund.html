Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: October 26, 2025

Thank you for choosing Ukobow. We value your trust and strive to provide a transparent experience. This policy outlines the terms regarding cancellations and refunds for hotel bookings made through our platform.

1. Cancellation Rules

Cancellation policies vary depending on the specific hotel and rate selected. However, the standard Ukobow platform rules are as follows:

  • Free Cancellation: Bookings cancelled at least 24 hours before the scheduled check-in time are eligible for a full refund (excluding platform convenience fees, if any).
  • Late Cancellation: Bookings cancelled within 24 hours of check-in will incur a charge equivalent to the first night's stay plus taxes.
  • Non-Refundable Rates: Some specific "Deal" or "Premium" rates are non-refundable. This will be clearly mentioned on the booking confirmation page.

2. No-Show Policy

If you do not check in on the scheduled date ("No-Show") and do not cancel the booking beforehand:

  • The reservation will be released automatically the next day.
  • No refund will be issued for the booking amount.

3. Refund Process & Timelines

If you are eligible for a refund according to the rules above:

  • Processing Time: Refunds are initiated by Ukobow within 24 hours of cancellation.
  • Bank Credit: The amount will reflect in your original payment source (Credit Card, UPI, or Bank Account) within 5 to 7 business days, depending on your bank's processing speed.
  • Ukobow Wallet: If you chose a wallet refund, it will be credited instantly.

4. Payment Gateway Charges

In cases of customer-initiated cancellations, the payment gateway transaction fees (approx 2%) may not be refunded, as these are levied by the banking partners.

5. Hotel-Specific Policies

Please note that Ukobow acts as an intermediary. Individual hotels may have specific policies during peak seasons (e.g., New Year, Holidays). The specific policy displayed on the Hotel Details page during booking takes precedence over this general policy.

6. Check-in Denials

If a hotel denies check-in due to a fault on their end (e.g., overbooking), Ukobow will:

  • Provide an alternative hotel of similar quality at no extra cost, OR
  • Issue a full 100% refund immediately.

Note: Refunds are not issued if check-in is denied due to lack of valid ID proof by the guest.
